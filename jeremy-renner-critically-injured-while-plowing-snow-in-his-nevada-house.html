    22 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Hollywood actor Jeremy Renner was brought into the hospital on Sunday in critical condition. He was severely injured after experiencing a weather-related accident while plowing snow in Nevada.

    The Washoe County Sheriff’s Office stated that Officers responded to a “traumatic injury” call in the area of Mount Rose Highway in Reno. 

    Jeremy was airlifted on Sunday to the hospital. His rep has confirmed that the actor is “in critical but stable condition.” Additionally, he said that Renner’s family is with and he is receiving excellent care.

    Renner has a daughter Ava Berlin from his ex-wife Sonni Pacheco. They divorced in 2015 and share custody of the daughter.

    Related: Complete information on daughter of Jeremy Renner

    Renner has a house 25 miles from Reno; it is near Mt. Rose-Ski Tahoe. In the new year, the region was hit by a winter storm affecting 35,000 houses across Northern Nevada’s Washoe, Carson, Douglas, Storey, and Lyon counties. The affected areas have lost power as of this morning.

    The Washoe County Sheriff’s Office Major Accident Investigation Team is investigating the circumstances.

    Jeremy Renner was nominated for Oscar for Best Actor for the movie The Hurt Locker in 2010 and Supporting Actor in 2011 for the movie The Town.

    He has been in numerous films; however, he is presently more known because of the Hawkeye character played in Marvel Cinematic Universe films. 

    His recent project was featured as Mike McLusky in the Mayor of Kingstown tv series.
