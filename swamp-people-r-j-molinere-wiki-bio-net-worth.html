    Career

    R.J. Molinere’s career in hunting can be traced back in 1979. Amazingly, it was his biological mother who taught him how to hunt alligators. According to wiki-bio, alligator hunting runs through his family line. Therefore, he started doing what his family does.

    He featured on the television for the first time in 2009. He was in a documentary movie called Pulling John. R.J. Molinere appeared the second time in 2011. This was the time he joined the cast of Swamp People on the History Channel.

    This is a reality TV series produced by Jay Peterson. It mainly focuses on a group of Louisiana people who hunt alligators.

    R.J. Molinere was co-starring together with Troy, Jacob Landry and others. At first, the hunters only focused on Louisiana’s swamp areas. However, as time went by, they extended into the Texas area.

    The show has since then seen a gradual growth. It has attracted a wide following being rated the best. It has made History Channel number one in the cable network.

    R.J. Molinere co-works with his son Jay Paul Molinere. They use airboats to access the different parts in the swamp.

    R.J. Molinere had a wrestling career which ran concurrently with hunting. He has a record winning several championships. He won The World Championship four times. This made him the 11th time National Champion.

    He also has a record of winning the Classic Arm Wrestling Championship twice.  His accolades did not end there. He also recorded a win of two GNC Championships held in Ottawa, Canada.
