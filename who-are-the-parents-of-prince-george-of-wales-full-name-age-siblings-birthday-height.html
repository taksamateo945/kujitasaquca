    In the enchanting realm of royal lineage, one family captures the world’s attention with their young heir, Prince George of Wales.

    As the third in line to the British throne, this little prince has become a global sensation, charming hearts with his adorable antics.

    While Prince George himself is a pint-sized celebrity, let’s take a closer look at the individuals who stand as the pillars of his world – the proud and doting parents who guide him through his royal journey.

    Meet the regal couple whose love story unfolds in the corridors of history, shaping the future of the British monarchy.

    Who are the parents of Prince George of Wales?

    The parents of Prince George of Wales are William, Prince of Wales, and Catherine, Princess of Wales.

    They are also known as the Duke and Duchess of Cambridge, titles they received from Queen Elizabeth II on their wedding day in 2011.

    William, Prince of Wales, is the eldest son of Charles, King of the United Kingdom, and the late Diana, Princess of Wales.

    He was born on June 21, 1982, at St Mary’s Hospital in London.

    He is second in line to the throne, behind his father.

    Catherine, Princess of Wales, is the eldest daughter of Michael and Carole Middleton, a former flight dispatcher and flight attendant, respectively.

    She was born on January 9, 1982, at Royal Berkshire Hospital in Reading.

    She met Prince William in 2001, when they were both students at the University of St Andrews in Scotland.

    What is Prince George of Wales’s full name?

    Prince George of Wales’s full name is George Alexander Louis.

    He was born on July 22, 2013, at St Mary’s Hospital in London, weighing 8lb 6oz.

    He is the first child of William and Catherine, and the third great-grandchild of Queen Elizabeth II.

    His name was announced on July 24, 2013, by Kensington Palace.

    Who are Prince George of Wales’s siblings?

    Prince George of Wales has two younger siblings:

    Princess Charlotte of Wales was born on May 2, 2015, at St Mary’s Hospital in London, weighing 8lb 3oz.

    She is the second child of William and Catherine, and the fourth great-grandchild of Queen Elizabeth II.

    Her name was announced on May 4, 2015, by Kensington Palace.

    Prince Louis of Wales was born on April 23, 2018, at St Mary’s Hospital in London, weighing 8lb 7oz.

    He is the third child of William and Catherine, and the fifth great-grandchild of Queen Elizabeth II.

    His name was announced on April 27, 2018, by Kensington Palace.

    When is Prince George of Wales’s birthday?

    Prince George of Wales’s birthday is on July 22.

    He will turn 10 years old in 2023.

    His birthday is usually celebrated privately with his family, but sometimes the public gets a glimpse of him on special occasions.

    For example, in 2016, he attended the Trooping the Colour parade with his parents and sister, where he waved to the crowds from the balcony of Buckingham Palace.

    In 2019, he joined his parents and siblings at a charity polo match, where he played with a toy polo mallet and a soccer ball.

    To mark his birthday, his parents often release new photos of him, taken by either themselves or professional photographers.

    For his ninth birthday in 2022, they shared a photo of him smiling on a Land Rover Defender, a tribute to his late great-grandfather Prince Philip, who died earlier that year.

    How old is Prince George of Wales in 2023?

    Prince George of Wales will be 10 years old in 2023.

    He was born on July 22, 2013, so he will celebrate his 10th birthday on July 22, 2023.

    What is Prince George of Wales doing in 2023?

    Prince George of Wales is attending Lambrook School in Berkshire, a prestigious prep school for boys and girls aged 3 to 13.

    He started there in September 2022, along with his sister Charlotte and brother Louis, after his family moved from London to Windsor.

    Lambrook School offers a wide range of academic, artistic, and sporting activities for its students, including:

    • filmmaking
    • animation
    • cookery
    • cricket
    • rugby
    • tennis
    • golf
    • swimming

    Rugby, soccer, and gymnastics are some of the things that interest Prince George.

    Prince George is also preparing for his future role as king, by:

    • learning about the history and traditions of the monarchy
    • accompanying his parents on some official engagements

    In 2023, he made his first solo appearance at the Royal International Air Tattoo, where he met with pilots and aircrew from around the world.

    How tall is Prince George of Wales?

    The exact height of Prince George of Wales is not publicly known, as the royal family does not release such information.

    However, based on his appearance in photos and videos, he seems to be above average height for his age.

    According to the NHS, the average height for a 10-year-old boy in the UK is 138.4 cm (4 ft 6.5 in).

    Prince George appears to be taller than that, as he is almost as tall as his mother, who is 5 ft 9 in.

    A rough estimate would put Prince George’s height at around 4 ft 8 in or 4 ft 9 in, but this is not confirmed by any official source.
